Modular Belt Conveyor

Modular belt conveyor is mainly composed of modular belts and steel frame support. Besides, there are other accessories like drive motor, control box, sprockets, brackets, guides, wear strips, leveling feet, tensioners, flanges, and so on.

Modular belt conveyors are a middle ground between belt conveyors and chain conveyors. A modular belt consists of individual plastic modules linked together with joint rods. This design allows it to handle hot workpieces and sharp-edged materials.

Plastic modular belts are easy to maintain, easy to clean, highly durable, and resistant to abrasion, making them ideal for industrial, food, pharma, and packaging applications.
